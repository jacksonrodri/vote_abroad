Virgin Island voters abroad need to request a ballot each calendar year you'd like to vote in. **[Click here](https://www.votefromabroad.org)** to start your ballot request now.

Check below for your delivery methods and deadlines, and to find your local election office contact information.

### Delivery Methods

* Registration: email, fax, or postal mail
* Ballot Request: email, fax, or postal mail
* Ballot Return: email, fax, or postal mail
