Washington voters abroad need to request a ballot each calendar year you'd like to vote in. [**Click here**](https://www.votefromabroad.org) to start your ballot request now.

Check below for your delivery methods and deadlines, and to find your local election office contact information.

### Delivery Methods

* Registration: email, fax, postal mail, or online~
* Ballot Request: not required
* Ballot Return: email, fax, or postal mail

~Registration: Washington's online voter registration system requires state identification
